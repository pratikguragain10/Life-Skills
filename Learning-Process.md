## 3. What are active and diffused modes of thinking?

- Active Mode of Thinking:

    - The active mode of thinking is when your mind is fully focused on a specific task.
    - You think logically and work step-by-step to find a solution to a problem.

- Diffused Mode of Thinking:

    - This mode occurs when the mind is relaxed and not focused on any task.
    - It helps generate creative ideas and solutions naturally, without pressure.
    - We are in a flow state.

## 4. what are the steps to take when approaching a new topic?

- Practice a concept regularly and repeatedly to understand it clearly. 
- Focus on deliberate pracitce for learning new concepts.
- Decide exactly what skill you want to achieve and break it into smaller steps.
- Practice the most important parts first.
- Learn to self-correct and improve your mistakes.
- Remove distractions and stay disciplined while working.
- Commit to practicing at least 20 hours.
- Ensure that all the resources and materials needed to learn the new topic are available.
