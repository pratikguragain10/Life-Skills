# Grit and Growth Mindest

---

## Question 1 (Grit)

### Summarize Grit Video.

- The pathway to success is through grit.
- Acquiring grit means sticking with your future day in day out-not just for week, not just for months, but for years-and working hard to make that future a reality.
- Talent doesn't make you gritty.
- To build grit, we need a growth mindset.

---

## Question 2 (Growth Mindset)

### Summarize Introduction to Growth Mindset Video.

- Mindests have major influence on people's ability to learn, and people who use a growth mindset tend to learn, grow, and achieve more over time than others.
- Always know that you can get better-no matter your age, your achievements, or what you’ve done in your career.
- Skills are built, so you can learn and grow.
- Focus on the process of getting better (Trust the Process).

---

## Question 3 (Understanding Internal Locus of Control)

### What is the Internal Locus of Control? What is the key point in the video?

- Internal Locus of Control, in my terms, is the belief that the factors we are able to control or handle-like hard work or extra effort—lead to our outcomes.
- Some of the key points from the video are mentioned below:
  - How much work you put into something is is completely in your control.
  - Take time to appreciate the fact that your actions solved the problems.
  - Have faith in the thought, "It was all because of ME!".
  - Build the belief that you are in control of your destiny.

  ## Question 4 (How to build a Growth Mindset)

  ### What are the key points mentioned by speaker to build growth mindset?

  - Believe in your ability to figure things out and get better.
  - Get up, get to work, and start working towards the dreams you want to achieve.
  - Question your assumptions.
  - Box in or narrow down your vision for your future.
  - Develop your own curriculum of life.
  - Architect a curriculum to make your dream possible.
  - Have an open mindset to change and to grow.
  - Honour the struggle when the journey gets hard and difficult.

  ---

  ## Question 5 (Mindset)

  ### What are your ideas to take action and build Growth Mindset?

  - Never let yourself down-always strive for greatness. It may take time, but trust the process.
  - Don't force yourself to do something that you don't understand at all.
  - The pace of progress doesn't matter; the success you get after the progress does.
  - Be responsible for your learning.
  - Always carry yourself with confidence.
  - Be focused on mastering any skillset you want to achieve or any work you are doing.
  - Treat new concepts and situations as learning opportunities.
  - Don’t fear or feel discomfort with doubts, confusion, or errors.
  - Break down problems into smaller parts and tackle them one at a time.
  - Try to learn from the mistakes so you can handle them if they come again.
  - Always be enthusiastic.
